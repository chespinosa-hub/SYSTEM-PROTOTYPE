Business Architecture

The system supports the major business processes of the Provincial Capitol Office including budget tracking, legislation management, and employee travel monitoring.

Budget Management

Centralized submission, validation, approval, and monitoring of provincial budgets.

Legislation Processing

Standardized drafting, approval, digital signing, and publishing of ordinances.

Travel Monitoring

Automated travel request, approval, fund validation, and liquidation process.

Information Architecture

All enterprise data are stored in a centralized relational database shared by PBTS, LLMS, and ETIMS.

Data Entity Description
Budget Proposal Department budget submissions and approval records (PBTS)
Ordinance / Resolution Legislative documents and status records (LLMS)
Travel Request Employee travel details, approvals, and liquidations (ETIMS)
User Account User roles such as Employees, Department Heads, and Officials
